Pasal 3 - HAK DAN KEWAJIBAN SISWA

HAK SISWA - STUDENT RIGHTS

  1. Participate in academic and non-academic activities conducted both inside and outside the school, including online and onsite formats.
  2. Receive religious education in accordance with Christian values.
  3. Utilize allschool facilities appropriately,according to theirintended functions and regulations.
  4. Receive equaltreatmentfromthe school principal,teachers, and staff.
  5. Access guidance and counseling servicesfromcounselors or teachers based on needs and procedures.
  6. Receive health services provided by the school clinic in accordance with needs and regulations.
  7. Respond to and defend against any allegations made toward them.
  8. Develop themselves by participating in Co-Curricular Activities(CCA) provided by the school.
  9. Develop themselves by participating in Co-Curricular Activities(CCA) provided by the school.
  10. Receive feedback or results on assignments or assessments within two weeks at the latest.
  11. Submit constructive suggestions or feedback to the school leadership, teachers, or staff in accordance with proper procedures and ethics.
  12. Participate in competitions or events on behalf of the school with the principal's approval.
  13. Receive information and take partin selection processesfor self-development opportunities (scholarships, competitions, student exchange programs).
  14. Receive report slips or diplomas regularly, provided all administrative payments and returns of borrowed school property have been fulfilled.
  15. Receive report card or diplomas upon withdrawal or graduation, provided that all administrative obligations and the return of borrowed property have been completed.

      16. KEWAJIBAN SISWA - STUDENT OBLIGATIONS

      1. Understand and uphold the school's core values: Love, Joy, Peace, Patience, Kindness, Goodness, Faithfulness, Gentleness, and Self-Control.
      2. Comply with allschool rules, both written and verbal.
      3. Show respect to all members of the school community through polite behavior and speech.
      4. Maintain the good reputation of the school both on and off campus, including on social media.
      5. Be present atschool on time during in-person learning.
      6. Participate in all classes and school programs.
      7. Informand request permission when absent;five consecutive unexcused absences are considered a withdrawal.
      8. Ask for clarification or updates on lessons or school activities that were missed or not understood.
      9. Come prepared with books and learning tools asrequired.
      10. Actively participate in school events and activities.
      11. Maintain cleanliness, a comfortable environment, and classroom order.
      12. Be responsible forschool property and replace any damage caused by negligence or intent.
      13. Take care of personal belongings
      14. Dress neatly and modestly according to the following standards:

        15. 14.1. Hari Senin hinggga kamis: Seragam putih biru

        Monday until Thursday :National Uniform(white-Blue)

        Standar Seragam Siswa Laki-laki / Male Students:
        • Shirtbuttoned and tucked into trousers
        • School tie
        • School cap during flag ceremony
        • Belt
        • School attributes(logo and name tag)

        Standar Seragam Siswi/ Female Students:

        • Blouse/shirt buttoned and tucked into skirt
        • Skirt below the knee
        • School cap during flag ceremony
        • School tie
        • School attributes(logo and name tag)

        14.2.Friday - Batik attire for both male and female students

        Requirements:

        • Collared shirt
        • Fabric pants or jeans, notleggings, gympants, or ripped jeans

        14.3.PE Day - School sports uniform

        • Must change into regular uniformafter PE class.
        • If PE is in the last period,students must wear their regular uniform first and change into PE clothes only when PE starts.
        • May wear PE uniform from home if PE is the first period

        14.4.Other activities - Smart casual attire

        • Top must have sleeves and not be see-through
        • Fabric pants or jeans(notleggings, gympants, or ripped jeans)
        • Any color shoes with socks must be worn
        • It is mandatory for students to wear shoes that adhere to school regulations; sandals are strictly prohibited within the school environment. Sandals may only be worn by students who are genuinely unwell and present a doctor's note or a letter from their parents
